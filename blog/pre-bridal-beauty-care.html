Pre-Bridal Beauty Care for Kerala Brides

By Nancy Sethumadhavan | Zanilia Beauty Parlor, Malappuram & Calicut

Every bride deserves to look and feel her best on her wedding day. That’s why Nancy Sethumadhavan has curated the ultimate pre-bridal beauty routine designed for Kerala’s unique climate and traditions.

1. Skincare Routine

Start at least 6–8 weeks before your wedding. Regular facials, exfoliation, and hydrating masks help you achieve that signature glass-skin glow.

2. Hair & Scalp Treatments

Healthy hair makes styling effortless. Our herbal and keratin hair spa sessions strengthen and smooth your hair for every function.

3. Body Polishing & Relaxation

Kerala brides love the traditional turmeric and sandalwood body packs for glowing skin. Combine this with full-body polishing and a relaxing aromatherapy massage.
